Chuckwagon Beans

Description

My mom used to make this every year at Christmas time. I'm saving the recipe here so I'll have it on hand.

Ingredients

    1/2 lb bacon (Optional)
    3 pound lean ground beef
    3 cups minced onion
    1 cup minced celery
    2 beef bouillon cubes
    2/3 cups boiling water
    2 cloves minced garlic
    1 ½ cups ketchup
    3 Tablespoons yellow mustard
    1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
    1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    2 cans (32oz each) baked beans with molasses

Steps

    Heat oven to 375 degrees.
    In Dutch oven, fry bacon until crisp; set aside. Drain fat from pan.
    In same pan, cook and stir ground beef, onion and celery until meat is brown and onion and celery are tender. (Drain excess fat if needed).
    Dissolve bouillon cubes in boiling water; stir bouillon and remaining ingredients into meat mixture.
    Cover; bake 1 hour 15 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Crumble bacon; sprinkle over beans.
