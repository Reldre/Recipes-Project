French Onion Dip

Description

This is my recipe for Frech Onion Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 250g Sour Cream
  • 250g Mayonaise
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Garlic Flakes
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Dried Parsley
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and carmalize it in a frying pan until dark brown, almost black
  2. Add all of the ingrediants into the container that will hold the dip and stir until well mixed
  3. Place the dip covered into the fridge for at least 1 hour and preferably 12 hours.
  4. Stir the dip one more time before serving
  5. The dip will keep for a week in the fridge assuming it's not all eaten by then
